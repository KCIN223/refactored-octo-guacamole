Lina Bo Bardi

1958

HOUSES OR MUSEUMS?


What should come first, houses or museums? Everything at once: the houses, the schools, the museums, the libraries...

Museum? What is a museum? In everyday life, when we want to describe a person, thing or idea that is outdated, not practical or useful, we often say ‘they belong in a museum’...

The problem of the museum has to be tackled today on ‘didactic’ and ‘technical’ grounds...

The experience gained in this field with the São Paulo Museum of Art can be of great use here...

These considerations are of the utmost importance as Bahia stands on the brink of creating what could well one day become the country’s most important museum...

First published in Diário de Notícias (Salvador, Bahia), 5 October 1958
